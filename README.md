## Research
My primary research interests lie in AI control and agent foundations, particularly understanding and mitigating misalignment risks in increasingly autonomous systems. I focus on empirical and theoretical questions around goal misgeneralisation, adversarial capability elicitation, and attack selection in agentic evaluations, aiming to clarify failure modes in frontier models. I am also interested in how these technical insights inform governance design, especially mechanisms for managing strategic risk and constraining dangerous capability deployment.
